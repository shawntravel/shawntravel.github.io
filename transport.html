How to go Pontian District from Singapore

The Pontian district is located in the southwestern part of Johor, about 1 hour from Johor Bahru, or about 1 hour 50 minutes from Singapore of driving time. There is some different methods of getting from Singapore to Pontian.

A) Getting to Pontian from Larkin Bus Terminal

There are 3 bus services that get you to Pontian from Larkin Bus Terminal - Kembara City, City Bus and Maju.
All three bus services have similar routes and operate on a daily basis.
