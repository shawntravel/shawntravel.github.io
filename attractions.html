Beautiful Attractions in Pontian

Have you ever explored the beautiful spots of Pontian yet? If you've been going to the same destinations during your free time, you must try different spot this time. Let us be your handy travel guide in exploring Pontian Town this time.

Taman Awam Tepi Laut

Another place to visit here is Taman Awam Tepi Laut. If you are fond of walking around or has a habit of jogging in exploring a place, you can do it here. Invite your best buddies and surely you will enjoy the beautiful view that you'll see here.

Kukup Laut

Visit Kukup Laut and be amazed for its two villages have a hundred years of history, also this is where the southernmost tip of Asian continent fishing village is located. You can also see here a village of tall house pillar standing in the marsh coast. You will probably think how creative the people in there in building a village above the seawater.
